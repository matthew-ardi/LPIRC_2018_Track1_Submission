Low-Power Image Recognition Challenge

the Challenge will be sponsored by other corporate and university sponsors, including but not limited to Google LLC

Agreement and will be entitled to enforce this Agreement as if they were parties to the Agreement.
Team Submissions:
The Team will create a submission in response to the Challenge. The Team will submit in response
to the Challenge one or more of the following: models, software code, software programs, hardware or other work product
